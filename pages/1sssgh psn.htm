Sri Sathya Sai General Hospital, Prasanthi Nilayam

This hospital was Sri Sathya Sai Baba’s first initiative in healthcare. Inaugurated on October 4th 1956, it was then, the only hospital in a 30 kilometre radius. Initially a primary care institution, it now offers both primary and secondary care. The infrastructure includes:

  • 94 beds.
  • 2 Operation Theatres.
  • 8 Wards (Pre and Post-operative).
  • 7 Departments – General Medicine, General Surgery,
  • Paediatrics, ENT, Ophthalmology, Dentistry, Obstetrics and Gynaecology.
  • Laboratory facilities.
  • Complement of Utility services.

In the past fifty years over 4 million patients have benefited from free healthcare services at this hospital.
